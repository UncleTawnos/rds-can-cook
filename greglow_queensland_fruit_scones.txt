Queensland Fruit Scones

Ingredients:
2 eggs
2 cups of mashed pumpkin
2 tablespoons of butter
1 cup of sugar
1/2 teaspoon of salt
4 1/2 cups of self-raising flour
1 cup of mixed fruit or sultanas or raisins

Preparation steps:
Let the mixed fruit soak in water over night
Let the mashed pumpkin cool before starting
Flour a board that can be used later for cutting the dough
Pre-heat oven to 250 degrees C

Baking steps:
Beat together butter, sugar and salt until light and fluffy
Add the eggs, then the pumpkin and fruit and stir in the flour
Spread the dough out on the board and cut into squares or circles
Place all scones on a tray on the top shelf of the oven
Cook for 15 minutes
